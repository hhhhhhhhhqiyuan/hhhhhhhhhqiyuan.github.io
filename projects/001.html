Acacia Outdoor Website

A commercial website system for Acacia Outdoor, built around brand presence, seasonal campaign storytelling, product discovery, and Shopify conversion.

Open live site View design system
theacacia.com / Space Acacia

Brief

Make outdoor gear feel modern, comfortable, and easy to shop.

The site presents Acacia's brand world, hero campaign, product families, roadshow promotion, press credibility, and product education. The homepage works as both a storefront and a seasonal marketing surface.

My Role

Commercial site direction and e-commerce experience.

This case study frames the work as a full e-commerce experience: homepage storytelling, product discovery, campaign modules, product detail architecture, proof content, and Shopify-ready conversion moments.

Homepage System

A storefront that behaves like a campaign landing page.

The homepage leads with a strong brand and seasonal campaign moment, then moves into product discovery, feature storytelling, press credibility, and shopping paths. It gives visitors a clear reason to explore before asking them to buy.

Hero campaign section creates a first impression around outdoor comfort and product desire. Product families and bundles support fast browsing across tents, floors, canopies, and accessories. Press and community sections add credibility before the visitor reaches deeper shopping flows.

Product Detail System

A product-first interface that explains before it sells.

The PDP starts with a recognizable Shopify product flow: image gallery, price, reviews, model selection, size, color, accessories, shipping promises, and cart actions. It keeps decision-making visible without making the page feel overloaded.

Hero gallery anchors the product visually before long-form storytelling begins. Variant controls turn model, size, and color into a guided decision path. Shipping, warranty, and financing signals reduce purchase friction early.

Commerce Flow

How the page moves a shopper from curiosity to purchase.

01Attract

Homepage campaign and brand visuals create the first reason to care.

02Explore

Product families, bundles, and navigation help shoppers find the right path.

03Decide

PDP modules explain model, size, features, materials, and use cases.

04Convert

Reviews, video, cross-sell, email capture, and add-to-cart complete the loop.

Story Modules

Feature education is treated like a campaign page.

Instead of relying only on product specs, the page uses editorial blocks and outdoor imagery to translate functional details into a more emotional buying story: comfort, durability, weatherproofing, and fast setup.

Dark and light sections create rhythm across a long product page. Large headlines turn technical benefits into memorable product claims. Image-led sections make the tent feel like a lifestyle system, not a SKU.

Retention Layer

The lower page turns proof into action.

After the product story, the page shifts into credibility and next steps: details, video, press/customer proof, recommendations, community signup, and footer navigation. This makes the product page work as both a sales page and a brand surface.

Video and review content give shoppers evidence after the feature story. Recommended products support basket expansion and category discovery. Community signup keeps the page useful even when a visitor is not ready to buy.
